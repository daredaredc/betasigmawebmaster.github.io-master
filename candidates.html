Candidates

This year we have 9 candidates for our chapter! Meet Meow Mix!

Annaliese Ford

Annaliese is a freshman studying Wildlife and has an incredible passion for the environment. Once she graduates, Annaliese hopes to help with the rehabilitation of endangered species.

Coming from Dyer, Indiana, Annaliese is currently playing string bass in the Symphony Orchestra.

In her spare time, Annaliese enjoys painting, cooking, reading, and being a part of the Zoo Club.

Annaliese has been welcomed into the Blue Celestials by her big, Jalan Miller.

Miranda McGuire

Miranda is a freshman studying Animal Science and has a passion for animal health. She hopes to use this passion, along with her time in 4-H and working on a Swine Farm, to research new and innovative practices for the animal health field.

From Greentown, Indiana, Miranda is now a part of the clarinet section in this year's Symphonic Band.

In her spare time, Miranda can be seen with the Block and Bridle Club, Foal Watch, and 4-H.

Miranda has been welcomed into the Daisies by her big, Jordan Bierman.

Erica Tyler

In her spare time, Erica enjoys watching movies, doing jigsaw puzzles, and hanging out with friends.

Erica has been welcomed into the Mixed Nuts by her big, AnaLisse Johansson.

Brad has been welcomed into the Cowbells by his big, Jared Boyden.
